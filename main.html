My Favorite Holiday Destination: Bali, Indonesia

Bali, Indonesia is a tropical paradise that has become a popular destination for tourists from all over the world. Known for its beautiful beaches, lush jungles, and vibrant culture, Bali has something to offer for everyone.

  1. Ubud Monkey Forest - a sacred monkey sanctuary in the heart of Ubud, where visitors can get up close and personal with friendly macaques.
  2. Mount Batur Sunrise Trek - a challenging but rewarding hike up to the summit of Mount Batur, where hikers can watch the sunrise over the island.
  3. Tegallalang Rice Terrace - a stunning terrace of rice paddies that stretch out as far as the eye can see.
  4. Uluwatu Temple - a cliff-top temple that offers breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean.
  5. Waterbom Bali - a water park with a variety of rides and attractions for visitors of all ages.
  • Nasi Goreng - a traditional Indonesian fried rice dish, usually served with a fried egg on top.
  • Sate - skewered and grilled meat, usually chicken or beef, served with a peanut sauce.
  • Babi Guling - a Balinese specialty of roasted suckling pig, typically served with rice and vegetables.
  • Gado-Gado - a salad made with boiled vegetables, tofu, and a peanut sauce dressing.
  • Nasi Campur - a mixed rice dish with various meats, vegetables, and spices.
